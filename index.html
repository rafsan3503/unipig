ABOUT US

$UNIPIG, developed after the launch of Uniswap, was a collaborative experiment between the Uniswap team and Plasma Group, designed to showcase the potential of Optimistic Rollups as a Layer 2 scaling solution for the Ethereum network. While it didn't lay the groundwork for Uniswap, UNIPIG played a crucial role in emphasizing the importance of user experience, scalability, and technological innovation in the DeFi space. Its interactive, gamified approach served not only as an educational tool but also as a persuasive testament to the community and developers alike that Ethereum and its applications can be both efficient and accessible, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in decentralized finance. $UNIPIG walked so that UNISWAP could run.
